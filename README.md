# YCal 2023

## Description
A calendar scheduling web application geared towards Yale students. Allows students to add the classes they are taking to their calendar, schedule their days, and mark events with specific tags such as club meetings, office hours, and social time with friends, along with Yale-specific locations! Includes a social friending system, where students can add their friends and view their availability along with what courses their friends are taking.
